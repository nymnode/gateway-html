This is a Nym Exit Gateway

Nym is a privacy-enhancing technology that allows users to communicate anonymously and securely over the internet. The Nym mixnet prevents adversaries from being able to monitor user traffic and trace it back to the sender. It does this by encrypting and routing data through multiple nodes in the network, ensuring complete privacy.

Nym Exit Gateways are operated by individuals committed to making the internet a safer place for everyone. By providing privacy through decentralized VPN-like services, they contribute to protecting users' online activities from surveillance, ensuring secure and anonymous communication.
